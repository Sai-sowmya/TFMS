Help & Support – Trade Finance Management System

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use each module effectively.

📄 How do I create a new Letter of Credit (LC)?

Go to the Letter of Credit module and click "Manage". Then select "Create New LC". Fill in the beneficiary details, amount, terms, and submit for approval. You can also amend or close existing LCs from the same section.

🏦 How do I issue or track a Bank Guarantee?

In the Bank Guarantee module, click "Manage" to view issued guarantees. Use "Create New" to initiate a new guarantee. You can track expiry dates, beneficiary info, and status updates from the dashboard.

📁 How do I upload trade documents?

Navigate to the Trade Documentation module and click "Manage". Use the upload feature to add invoices, shipping documents, contracts, and other relevant files. All documents are stored securely and can be searched by transaction ID.

⚠️ How is risk assessed in TFMS?

The Risk Assessment module analyzes transaction history, counterparty ratings, and exposure limits. Click "Assess" to generate a risk score and view recommendations. This helps in making informed decisions before proceeding with a trade.

✅ What is required for compliance?

In the Compliance module, upload KYC documents, trade licenses, and audit reports. The system checks for alignment with international trade regulations. You’ll be notified of any missing or outdated documents.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Tips
  • If a module doesn't load, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
  • Ensure you're logged in with the correct role (e.g., Admin, Trader, Auditor) to access specific modules.
  • For file upload issues, check that your document format is supported (.pdf, .docx, .xlsx).
